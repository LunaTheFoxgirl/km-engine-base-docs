A Sound object is a sound effect.

NOTE: These are all loaded uncompressed in to memory If you want to play long form music *use* Music

Otherwise your game can use like, over 1 GB of ram.

NOTE: If you want a sound to play in some 3D space it needs to be mono!

NOTE: Only ogg files are supported currently, you can use VLC Media Player or other tools to convert audio to ogg. Ogg is also called Ogg Vorbis or Vorbis.
